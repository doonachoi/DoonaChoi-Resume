As a designer, I have had a few experiences with designing a variety of designs for labels, postcards, stickers, and illustrations. Still learning more about designing and drawing illustrations, my mind and will are open to any opportunities that lie ahead of me. First, starting with designing posters and announcements for my school club, I started to build my interest and experience with designing. Outside of school, I started graphic designing more posters and notifications for extracurricular clubs such as volunteer organizations and church activities. After realizing the growth in my interest and skills I started looking and working more into this field.

After graduating high school, I first started working more professionally within the designing field as I starting drawing illustrations for magazines at my church. With a large population within the church, Our church has a media team that uploads and prints magazines for the church. Within the magazine contains church news and announcements. From the designing manager in the team, I was offered the role of designing illustrations that would appear within the pages throughout the magazine. It has been about a year since I have started and continue to design illustrations for each term.

Starting my role as an illustration designer, I started finding more interest in designing my own products. Including stickers, postcards, pouches, and cases, I started creating products with my designs printed on them. Creating different designs constantly, my skills and experience have increased with drawing designs electronically.
